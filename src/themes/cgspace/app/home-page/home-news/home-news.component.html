Welcome

CGSpace helps you identify, locate and download research and other outputs produced across the CGIAR and its partners. The repository has information on reports, articles, press releases, presentations, videos, policy briefs, datasets, infographics and more. As well as this knowledge discovery service, the repository supports reporting across CGIAR initiatives and platforms.

Content is organized in communities and collections and includes current and historical outputs from:

  • CGIAR’s current research initiatives and impact platforms
  • The CGIAR System Organization and Independent Advisory and Evaluation Service
  • Collaborative projects like AICCRA, PABRA and the CGIAR AMR hub
  • Complete outputs of CGIAR research centers – AfricaRice, the Alliance of Bioversity International and CIAT, the iInternational Institute of Tropical Agriculture, the International Livestock Research Institute, the International Potato Center, and the International Water Management Institute
  • Selected outputs from other centers - the International Center for Agricultural Research in the Dry Areas, the International Food Policy Research Institute, the International Maize and Wheat Improvement Center, the International Rice Research Institute and WorldFish, as well as the Center for International Forestry Research
  • Legacy content from several CGIAR research programs and platforms (2011-2021) and other system-wide as well as a historic archive of CGIAR documentation
  • Some hosted content of other organizations and programs

To help you navigate, you can:

  • BROWSE the communities and collections
  • SEARCH for concepts or people using free or controlled terms and filters
  • EXPLORE, visualize and generate reports across the repository
